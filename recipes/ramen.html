Description

This is a simple, budget ramen recipe for people who lowkey can't cook (it's ok bro me too).

Ingredients

  • Instant Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Put instant noodles in a bowl.
  3. Open any seasoning packets and dump them in the bowl.
  4. Pour recently boiled water in the bowl.
  5. Wait approximately 15 minutes.
  6. Stir.
  7. Enjoy!
