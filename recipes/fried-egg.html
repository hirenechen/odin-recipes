荷包蛋 Recipe

Homepage

Description

This is a simple, budget fried egg recipe for people who hate when the yolk in their egg is runny.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Salt (Optional)
  • Pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat frying pan.
  2. Crack the egg into the pan.
  3. Mix around the yolk and whites, if desired.
  4. When the egg is cooked sufficiently on one side, flip it with a spatula.
  5. When fully cooked, turn off the stove.
  6. Add salt and pepper, if desired.
  7. Enjoy!
