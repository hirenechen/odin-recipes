Description

This is a simple, budget sushi recipe for avocado enthusiasts.

Ingredients

  • Seaweed
  • Rice
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Cut the avocado into chunks.
  2. Take out a sheet of seaweed.
  3. Use a spoon to put rice on one half of the seaweed.
  4. Use a spoon to put avocado chunks on the other side of the seaweed.
  5. Starting from the avocado side, roll the seaweed up.
  6. Use scissors or a knife to cut the sushi.
  7. Enjoy with soy sauce!
